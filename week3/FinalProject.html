Corgi, a.k.a. "Dwarf Dog" - In Welsh, “cor” means “dwarf” while “gi” is a form of the word “dog,” so their name literally translates to "dwarf dog".
Legend has it that fairies and elves once used corgis to pull their coaches and act as fairy cattle herders.
These little fluffy bundles of joy are known to be extremely affectionate dogs and love themselves some cuddles.
